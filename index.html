NoLACE Demo Samples

This page hosts demo samples for the Non-Linear-Adaptive Coding Enhancer (NoLACE) at bitrates 6, 9 and 12 kb/s. The samples were taken from the CMU ARCTIC dataset (see this for description and license) and from the VCTK dataset (provided under creative commons license). Headphones are recommended for listening.
